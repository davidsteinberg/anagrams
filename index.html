What is an anagram?


An anagram is a gram', Nana. Hehehe.

It is a word (or words) made from shuffling up the letters in a different word (or words).

What should I do?


1.  Type some text in the box and click Go!
2.  Choose from the anagrams it produces to make cool word chains.


To save a word you like:

    Click on the word or drag it to the Saved Words section.

To get rid of a word you have saved and reuse its letters in your anagrams:

    Click on the word or drag it to the Anagrams section.

To get rid of all words you have saved and reuse all letters:

    Click the recycling bin.


You can rearrange the words you have saved by dragging and dropping them.
